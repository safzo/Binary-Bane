Technical Teardown: Supply Chain ROI

Situation: $150k annual loss in overstocking due to naive moving-average forecasting.

Task: Build a system that accounts for lead-time volatility.

Action: Implemented a Bayesian Inventory Model using PyMC3 to simulate demand distributions.

Result: 20% reduction in capital lock-up within 4 months.

Technical Teardown: Financial Risk

Situation: High volatility in debt interest payments affecting cash flow predictability.

Task: Quantify the 'Value at Risk' (VaR) for the portfolio.

Action: Built Monte Carlo simulations to stress-test 10,000 market scenarios.

Result: Predicted 95% of outliers, allowing for a strategic hedge that saved $40k.
