System Documentation & Privacy

Effective Date: March 21, 2026

01. Data Philosophy

As a data professional, I value minimal entropy and maximum privacy. This site is designed to be a "Zero-Knowledge" platform. I do not track your behavior, sell your information, or use invasive advertising pixels.

02. Infrastructure & Hosting

This website is architected using GitHub Pages. While I do not personally store your data, GitHub may collect standard system logs (including IP addresses and browser types) to ensure server security and performance. For more details, refer to the GitHub Privacy Statement.

03. Cookies & Tracking

This site does not utilize first-party tracking cookies. Third-party assets (such as Font Awesome icons) are loaded via Cloudflare CDN. These providers may process technical metadata to deliver files efficiently to your region.

04. External Connections

Clicking links to LinkedIn, GitHub, or Discord will redirect you to third-party environments. Once you leave safzo.github.io, your data is governed by the privacy protocols of those respective platforms.
