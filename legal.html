01. Data Philosophy

This site is architected as a "Zero-Knowledge" platform. I value minimal entropy and maximum privacy. I do not track your behavior, sell your information, or utilize invasive advertising pixels.

02. Infrastructure

Hosted via GitHub Pages. While I do not personally store your data, GitHub may collect standard system logs (IP addresses/browser types) for security. See the GitHub Privacy Statement.

03. Cookies & Tracking

No first-party tracking cookies are utilized. Third-party assets (Font Awesome) are delivered via Cloudflare CDN, which may process technical metadata to optimize regional file delivery.

04. External Nodes

Links to LinkedIn, GitHub, or Discord redirect to external environments. Once you leave safzo.github.io, your data is governed by the protocols of those respective platforms.
